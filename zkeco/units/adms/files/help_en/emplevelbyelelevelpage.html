The personnel elevator levels set.


To assign elevator levels for the personnel to verify and get through, personnel elevator levels have two display modes:ï¼š


Display as levels: Add/delete personnel for specific elevator levels.


Display as personnel: Add specified personnel into specified elevator levels, or delete specified personnel from specified elevator levels;


Add/delete personnel to levels


(1)Click [Elevator control] - [Personnel elevator levels] - [Show by Elevator levels], and click a level, then personnel having opening levels in the elevator level will be shown in the list on the right;


(2)Click [Add personnel] to pop up the Add personnel interface.


Select personnel to create the list on the right, and click [OK] to complete adding, and added personnel will appear in the list on the right;


(3)Select personnel, click [Delete from elevator level] to delete the personnel from the elevator level.


    Note: When adding personnel, if selected personnel exist in the current elevator level, the system can not add again.
