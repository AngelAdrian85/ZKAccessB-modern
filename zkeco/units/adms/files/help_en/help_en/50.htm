8.5 Personal Elevator Levels Distribution

Choose【Elevator Control System】→ 【Personal Elevator Levels】→【Show by Access Levels】, to view all elevator control rights groups. Select a rights group to view all members in the group, and add or delete members to or from the group.

 

 

Click【Elevator Control System】→【Personal Elevator Levels】→【Show by Personal】, click【Show by Personal】, to view all personnel. Select an employee to view the rights group to which the employee belongs and add or delete the employee to or from a group.
