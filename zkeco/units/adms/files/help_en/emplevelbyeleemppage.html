The personnel elevator levels set.


To assign elevator levels for the personnel to verify and get through, personnel elevator levels have two display modes:ï¼š


Display as levels: Add/delete personnel for specific elevator levels.


Display as personnel: Add specified personnel into specified elevator levels, or delete specified personnel from specified elevator levels;


Edit elevator level for personnel


(1)Click [Elevator control] - [Personnel elevator level settings] - [Shown by personnel] interface, click a person, and the list on the right will show the elevator level of the person;


(2)Click [Add elevator level] to pop up edit interface, select elevator level, click [OK] to complete editing, and the list on the right will show the elevator level;


(3)Select elevator level, and click [Delete elevator level] to the person from the elevator level.
